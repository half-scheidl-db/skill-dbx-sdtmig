# CDISC Therapeutic Area User Guides (TAUGs)

> **Source:** <https://www.cdisc.org/standards/therapeutic-areas>
> **Retrieved:** 2026-04-01

Therapeutic Area User Guides (TAUGs) extend the CDISC Foundational Standards to represent data that pertains to specific disease areas. TAUGs include disease-specific metadata, examples and guidance on implementing CDISC standards for a variety of uses, including global regulatory submissions.

## Summary

- **Total Therapeutic Areas:** 47
- **Standards covered:** Terminology, SDTM, CDASH, ADaM

## Notes

- All 47 TAUGs include SDTM guidance.
- 45 of 47 include Controlled Terminology.
- 28 of 47 include CDASH (data collection) guidance.
- 27 of 47 include ADaM (analysis dataset) guidance.
- TAUGs with full standards coverage (Terminology + SDTM + CDASH + ADaM) are the most mature for end-to-end implementation.
- Two TAUGs (Diabetes Type 1 Screening and Rare Diseases) do not list a specific version on the CDISC site.
- This file is a reference index only. The full TAUG documents require CDISC membership or purchase.
